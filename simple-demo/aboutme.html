About Me

Meghan is a level 19 wizard. She currently spends her time at Harvard Wizarding University where she practices the dark arts of biomedical engineering. In her quest for sagacious enlightenment, she works in her guild, Global Alliance for Medical Innovation, where she combines the magicks of biology and computer science to provide an early mobile diagnostic for malaria in impoverished regions of the world. Meghan resides beyond the misty mountains in lovely Lynn, Massachusetts, with her mythical sphynx, Slinky.
